Our Story

Serving Farmers Since 1995

Vishal Beej Bhandar was founded in 1995 by Shri Ramakant Singh in Naya Bhojpur, Dumraon, with a simple mission — to give the hardworking farmers of Buxar access to genuine, high-quality seeds and crop inputs at honest prices.

What began as a small seed counter has grown into one of the most trusted agri-input stores in the region. As an authorised dealer of leading brands like Indofil, Seminis and Clause, we ensure every product on our shelf is 100% genuine and quality-checked.

For us, it's never just been about selling seeds — it's about standing beside the farmer through every season, every harvest, every challenge.
